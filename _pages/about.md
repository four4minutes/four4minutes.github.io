I am a Ph.D. student at Yokohama National University, working in natural language processing.

My research focuses on helping AI systems, especially large language models, handle real-world knowledge more accurately and flexibly. I am interested in information extraction, retrieval-augmented generation, and knowledge graph construction as ways to reduce hallucination, improve consistency, and support more reliable reasoning over complex technical and factual documents.
