This paper proposes a method for automatically generating Why-type question-answer datasets for evaluating RAG systems. Because manually constructing high-quality QA datasets is costly, especially in domains that require expert knowledge, the method uses the Failure Knowledge Database as a source for generating evaluation data. Experiments showed that the proposed approach can generate datasets that follow the Why-question format and maintain a practical level of quality, supporting more scalable evaluation of domain-specific RAG systems.
