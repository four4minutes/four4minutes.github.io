This paper describes the Forst team's systems for the NTCIR-15 QA Lab-PoliInfo-2 task, including stance classification, dialog summarization, and entity linking for Japanese political information.

Younghun Lim's contribution was centered on the entity linking component. The entity linking task extracted mentions of legislation from Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly minutes and linked them to Wikipedia pages. Because this setting focuses on legislation rather than general entities, the system had to handle domain-specific challenges such as variant expressions for the same law, abbreviations, composite mentions, and phrases that resemble legal entities but should not be linked. The proposed pipeline combined key phrase filtering, mention detection, binary classification, and dictionary-based linking, achieving the best reported entity linking F-score of 0.3912 among the submitted Forst runs.
